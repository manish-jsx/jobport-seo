Search with Backfills!

Search with Backfills for your next career opportunity or star candidate. Founded in 2014, Backfills is a well-trusted boutique recruitment agency based in Malta. Our team is a group of multi-skilled hardworking consultants whose aim is to find the right match for you. Our approach to recruitment is holistic, using skills-based matching as well as taking culture and values into consideration.

Backfills forms part of a group of companies that has over 25 years’ experience in HR, Training, and Coaching. We aim to make your experience with us as a candidate or employer valuable; taking into consideration your needs, time limits, and potential to increase your capabilities.

We also keep fully updated with employment laws and trends to remain as reliable and professional as is possible. Get in touch with us today to see how we can help maximise your career or team’s potential!

Our Values

Passion

Our passion is helping our clients and candidates find the right match. By the right match, we mean holistically, not just a financial fit. Helping people and companies progress is what makes us feel great about what we do. 

Growth

We embrace opportunities to learn and grow. We invest in our team's development so that we can contribute to yours. Growth is never easy, but overcoming the challenges makes us who we have become today. 

Collaboration

We succeed by helping others. We get there by you getting there. When you give people the opportunity to achieve, they do great things. 

Quality

We believe that quality is the result of our effort as a team on a daily basis. Every moment is an opportunity to improve. By providing the right support system to our team, we are able to learn and develop and offer the same support to our clients and candidates. 

Transparency

Transparency is everything! We promote an open dialogue within a collaborative environment. Integrity is the foundation on which we have built Backfills. Your trust in us is paramount. Everything we do is centered around transparency and building honest relationships with our clients and candidates. 

Fun

What's life without a little fun! Fun at work is crucial. No, it's not possible every minute of the day, but it's crucial to feel happy, positive, motivated, and have those fun moments that bond everyone as a team. Whether it's a moment of craziness or a planned team meet, fun is a cherished part of our Backfills family.
